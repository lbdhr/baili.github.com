项目经历:

  • Participated in the Project of National Social Science Foundation of China: Evaluation on the impact of urban transport policy and infrastructure construction on atmospheric environment.

  • Used portable environmental monitoring equipment to collect pollutant data in Hong-Qiao Hub and carried out Generalized Additive Model (GAM) to find out the distribution of air pollutants in the transportation hub.
  • Participated in the cooperative project of Shanghai Jiao Tong University and eleme.com: Safety feasibility study about UAV takeaway distribution system.
